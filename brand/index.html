Assessment Limitations
This assessment covers publicly visible content at the time of scan. Our crawler may not have accessed every page on this domain — evidence hosted on subdomains, gated pages, or JavaScript-rendered sections may not be captured. A score reflects what was visible at scan time; it does not assess product safety, efficacy, or regulatory compliance. If relevant evidence was missed, brands can submit it via Brand Portal for review. Scores are updated when evidence becomes verifiably visible on the live page.
