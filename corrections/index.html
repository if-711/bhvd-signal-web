What we can correct
We will update scores for:
Public evidence pages our crawler missed
Claims that changed since the scan date
Misread or misclassified claim text
Wrong product category assignment
We do not change scores based on:
Evidence not publicly visible on your live page
Unpublished research or private studies
Disagreement with methodology weights or thresholds
Evidence submitted only to us and not visible on your site
