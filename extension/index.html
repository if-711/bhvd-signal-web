How it works
Three steps. Two seconds.
No manual lookup required. The extension does the match automatically as you browse.
01 — BROWSE

Visit any wellness product page

Navigate to a supplement, skincare, or wearable product page as you normally would. The extension runs in the background — no button to click.

02 — MATCH

Extension checks the NORM index

The extension reads visible page text — brand name, product claims, evidence references. It sends this to the NORM API, which matches against our scored index. No browsing history is stored.

03 — SIGNAL

Get the score while you decide

A sidebar appears with the brand's full report — signal color, score, dimension breakdown, and per-claim receipts. Knowledge before the money moves.

Privacy
We read claims, not people.
The extension reads product page text. That's it. No personal data, no identity, no tracking.
✓

Only reads health & wellness pages

The extension only activates on pages our classifier identifies as wellness product pages. It doesn't run on your bank, your email, or any other site.

✓

Claims text only — never your identity

The extracted page text is sent to the NORM API. Your name, email, location, and any personal data stay on your device.

✓

No browsing history stored

We don't log which pages you visit. The API receives the text and domain of the current page — nothing else.

✓

No third-party data sharing

Scan data is never sold to brands, advertisers, or data brokers. We're consumer-first by design — brands don't fund this index.
