Compliance statement

"Paid access never influences public Sigñal scores, rankings, or review outcomes."

Sigñal scores are the output of editorial review of publicly available brand marketing claims and visible evidence. Reviewers do not have access to commercial account information when scoring. Brand Pro subscribers receive workflow tools and analytical access to their own data — not editorial access, queue priority, or score modification capabilities. This separation is enforced structurally, not by policy alone.

Verified Access — Transparency Layer

Evidence submission, independently reviewed.

Brands may submit non-public evidence for Signal's editorial review. Verified Access is a transparency tool — not a marketing channel. A Verified Access badge may appear on a brand page if evidence clears review. The public Evidence Gap Score is never modified by Verified Access.

Brands submit non-public evidence

Studies, certifications, regulatory filings, or internal validation data not publicly indexed.

Signal reviews independently

Editorial team assesses submitted evidence against the same rubric used for all public scoring.

Verified Access badge may be displayed

If evidence clears review, a Verified Access indicator appears on the brand page — separate from the score.

Public Evidence Gap Score is not modified

Verified Access does not raise, lower, or influence the public score. Scores reflect public marketing claims only.
