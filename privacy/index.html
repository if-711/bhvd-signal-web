Privacy Policy

Effective: May 2026  ·  NORM Analytics

Short version: We collect your email and what you search for. We use it to run your account and show you relevant scores. We do not sell your data, advertise against it, or share it with brands you are looking up.

1. Who we are

NORM Analytics operates trynorm.co. We are the data controller for information collected through the NORM service. For questions: support@trynorm.co.

2. What we collect

DataWhenWhy
Email address Account creation, waitlist signup Authentication, account recovery, service communications
Brand searches When you search or request a scan To improve coverage and understand which brands matter to users
Saved brands & alerts When you add to your watchlist To deliver score-change alerts you request
Subscription data If you purchase a paid plan Billing, access management, receipts
Auth token (local storage) On login Keeps you signed in across sessions
Basic usage data During site use Bug fixes and performance monitoring; not behavioural advertising

We do not collect precise location data. We do not build advertising profiles. We do not read your health records, fitness data, or any data beyond what you directly provide to NORM.

3. How we use your data

  • Account management — authentication, subscription access, support
  • Score-change alerts — notifying you when brands on your watchlist are re-scored
  • Coverage decisions — aggregated, anonymised brand search demand helps us decide which brands to score next
  • Service communications — onboarding, significant product changes, billing events. Not marketing newsletters unless you opt in.
  • Security — detecting abuse, fraud, and unauthorised access

We do not use your data for advertising, do not sell it to data brokers, and do not share it with the brands you are researching.

4. Third parties we use

ServicePurposeData shared
Supabase Authentication, database, file storage Email, account data, saved brands
Stripe Payment processing (paid plans) Payment details (handled entirely by Stripe; we see billing status only)
Fly.io API server hosting Request logs (IP, endpoint, timestamp) — retained 30 days

These services are bound by their own privacy policies and by contractual data processing agreements. We select sub-processors that maintain industry-standard security practices.

5. Cookies and local storage

NORM uses browser local storage to keep you signed in between sessions (auth token). We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising pixels. Clearing your browser's local storage will sign you out.

6. Data retention

Account data is retained while your account is active and for up to 90 days after deletion (to allow recovery in case of error). Anonymised, aggregated demand signals (which brands were searched) are retained indefinitely — these cannot be linked back to individual users. Server request logs are deleted after 30 days.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction — update inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request erasure of your account and associated personal data
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Object — opt out of any processing you do not consider necessary

To exercise any of these rights, email support@trynorm.co with the subject "Privacy Request". We will respond within 30 days.

You can delete your account at any time from your account page.

8. Children

NORM is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Security

We use industry-standard practices: encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), access controls, and separation of production and non-production systems. No system is perfectly secure — if you suspect unauthorised access to your account, contact us immediately.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be communicated by email to registered users at least 14 days before taking effect. The effective date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

11. Contact

Privacy questions and requests: support@trynorm.co

General contact: NORM Analytics, support@trynorm.co
